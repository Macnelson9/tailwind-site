About Grocify

Founded in 2023, Grocify has rapidly become a leading grocery delivery service, dedicated to bringing fresh and quality groceries to your doorstep in just 15 minutes. Since our inception, we have successfully fulfilled over 500,000 orders, serving more than 120,000 satisfied customers across the nation. Our reliable delivery network covers over 30 states, ensuring that communities everywhere have access to fresh produce, meats, dairy, baked goods, and essential non-perishable items. Through our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, Grocify has achieved over 3 million dollars in profits, reflecting our strong growth and trusted reputation in the industry.
